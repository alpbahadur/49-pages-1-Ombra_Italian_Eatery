Experience Modern Italian Excellence

In the vibrant heart of Santurce, San Juan, Puerto Rico, Ombra Italian Eatery offers an unparalleled dining experience where authentic Italian tradition meets contemporary innovation.

Led by Michelin-starred Chef Alfredo Esposito, we bring you the finest flavors of Abruzzo with a Mediterranean soul, crafted from the freshest local ingredients.

Chef Alfredo Esposito

A Legacy Born in Abruzzo

Chef Esposito's culinary heritage spans 130 years, rooted in the rich traditions of Abruzzo, Italy, where his family's passion for authentic Italian cuisine began.

Michelin-Starred Excellence

Bringing world-renowned expertise and innovative techniques honed through years of Michelin-starred experience across Europe and South America.

A New Chapter in Puerto Rico

Since 2020, Chef Esposito has been redefining high-end Italian cuisine in Puerto Rico, blending traditional recipes with Mediterranean influences and local ingredients.

Signature Creations

Each dish tells a story of tradition, innovation, and the finest ingredients

Handmade Pastas

Crafted daily with traditional techniques, featuring fresh seasonal ingredients and authentic Italian flavors.

Mediterranean Seafood

Fresh catches prepared with olive oil, herbs, and time-honored methods from the Italian coastline.

Artisan Desserts

Exquisite sweet creations that honor Italian pastry traditions with a modern, elegant presentation.
